frozen yogurt

Frozen yogurt was invented in the United States in the 70's as a healthier , low-fat alternative to ice cream and since then has grown tremendously in the United States and Asia. Europe follows at a rapid pace and the last few years here one discovers the freshness of frozen yogurt !

Healthy , fresh and delicious taste

The oyya frozen yogurt is pure indulgence. The frozen yogurt is prepared on site at the time of your order with skim milk and nonfat yogurt. It is a refreshing delicacy with a wonderful flavor and creaminess. Oyya frozen yogurt is low in fat, less sugar and packed with probiotics to make a healthy snack. You have a daily choice of two flavors of yogurt and more than twenty toppings such as fresh fruit, chocolates, cookies , nuts and sweets. Then you may choose a sauce to complete your oyya frozen yogurt experience!
