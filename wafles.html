Liege waffles

Legend has it that the Liège waffle was invented in the 18th century by the cook of the Prince of Liege. To please him , the cook tried in a waffle iron to make a cake made with chunks pearl sugar mixed with vanilla dough. This creation was an immediate success , sugar waffle was born and belonged very quickly to the culinary traditions of Belgium .

At oyya Liege waffles are baked fresh daily . Oyya Liege waffles contain only superior quality ingredients: pearl sugar , wheat flour, vegetable fat , eggs, natural vanilla ... His yeast dough makes for a light and soft texture inside with a crispy exterior, browned as desired. Delicious pieces of pearl sugar is melted inside and delicious caramelized outside .
